Information you may share

If you use the website contact form or call the clinic, you may choose to share details such as your name, phone number, email address, and a short message about your concern.

How website inquiries may be used

Information shared through the website may be used to respond to questions, follow up about inquiries, and help direct patients to the appropriate next step. Appointments are booked by phone only.

Medical information

Visitors should avoid sending highly sensitive medical information through general website forms unless specifically requested by the clinic. For urgent medical concerns, contact appropriate emergency services instead of relying on the website.

Third-party tools

This website may include third-party tools such as Google Maps, fonts, or embedded content that may collect technical information according to their own policies.

Policy updates

Website content and policies may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the website indicates acceptance of the current version of this policy.
